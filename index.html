About ACM VVIT

Welcome to Our Chapter

The ACM VVIT Student Chapter is a vibrant community of computing enthusiasts at vasireddy venkatadri institute of technoogy. We are dedicated to fostering technical excellence, innovation, and professional growth among our members.

As part of the world's largest educational and scientific computing society, we bring together students, faculty, and industry professionals to share knowledge, develop skills, and explore the latest trends in computing technology.

Our chapter organizes various activities including technical workshops, coding competitions, hackathons, guest lectures, and networking events to help students grow both academically and professionally.
